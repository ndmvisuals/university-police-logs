Opening up University of Maryland
police logs

Examine arrest logs reported by the University of Maryland Police Department. The data is released on the department's website, spread out across multiple pages.
This dashboard scrapes the website every morning at 9 a.m. Aggregating the arrest logs into one source allows for exploration of patterns by year, incident type, and race.

Methodology:

The University of Maryland Police Department publishes daily crime and incident logs, which provide basic information on all calls for service the police department responds to. Capital News Service built a web scraper to parses through and collects this data every day and displays it on this dashboard.

Incident type comes from matching UMPD case numbers to crime log. However, if the incident is not included in the crime log, it is classified as N/A.
